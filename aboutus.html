Our Story


For thousands of tech professionals in some of the most vibrant tech markets in the US, Built In is the best place to learn about the industry, build connections at events and carve out futures at companies they believe in.

THE START

Built In was founded in 2011 on a love of Ahmedabad, its people and tech — as a social network and blogging platform. We gave local startups a sense of community and platform on which to tell their stories.



LIGHT BULB MOMENT

We learned that startups were attracting talent just by participating in our community. Refocusing our energies, we built a new model for tech recruitment to harness the power of content and community.



A REVOLUTION IN RECRUITMENT

Today, our content and events give tech professionals insights into hiring companies — the kind no job board could ever deliver. We serve seven US tech communities, with plans for expansion on the horizon.



As we grow, we remain committed to our origins. In everything we do, we focus on uniting tech talent and the tech industry — two forces that are powerful alone but invincible together.
